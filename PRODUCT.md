# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students, candidates, and learners preparing for exams (e.g., "Calon Taruna"). They need a focused, distraction-free environment to take tryouts and track their progress.

## Product Purpose
A functional dashboard to manage and execute exams (Tryouts), review results, and manage user profiles.

## Brand Personality
Professional, modern, clear, and focused. It should feel like a premium, reliable tool for serious preparation.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, enterprise-heavy dashboards with too many nested menus.
- "SaaS cream" monoculture with low-contrast gray text.
- Sketchy or overly whimsical illustrations.

## Design Principles
- Clarity over decoration: Data and actions should be immediately obvious.
- Focused execution: The exam-taking experience should be front and center.
- Premium feel: High contrast, purposeful motion, and clean typography.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High contrast for readability (WCAG AA), accessible form controls, clear focus states for keyboard navigation.
